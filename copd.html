Design Brief

How to turn healthcare services into “collaborative wellness”?

Neighborly is a nationwide senior care service that provides trained caregivers to seniors and disabled adults who need assistance to stay independent in the home. We are working to help them understand the realities of home management of COPD that will be faced by their non-medically trained caregivers. They needs to build a training program that prepares caregivers to support these patients and their lived reality, not just their disease.
