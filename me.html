Hi! I'm Yenan /'jenʌn/. People call me Sophie. I'm a designer specialized in Product and Service Design, while I also have the side knowledge and experience in Research, Design Strategy, and Development.

I decided to become a designer since I was in high school. Because I want to make full use of my creativity which is a natural gift of me. I spend 6 years at school learning and practicing on design. There are lots of tears and sweat throughout the process. But I gradually cleared my way and decide to take Product Design as my career path.

I'm bold. I'm fearless. My life is mostly about pushing the boundaries of my comfort zone. Because of that, I always fail, while I always get to learn quickly and vividly. The lucky thing is that I got to learn how to be disciplined and practical during my work with other people. And also the importance of combining these two mindsets.

I hope I could grow and become a Designer who has a bigger voice. I also hope that, at some day, I could be experienced enough and bold enough to lead the industry and explore the boundaries of Design in the near future.
