Ι  Matt and Me

Shindy is about a mobile payment solution targeted at the bar industry. We are aiming to build a smooth and enjoyable experience for bar-lovers as well as bar-tenders.

The project began roughly 6 months ago.

Our beta prototype is on track to be finished mid-October.

The v1 release to friends and family is scheduled for end of November. In this release, we’ll include the ability to open a bar tab, see your current tab, close it (Pay by card or apple pay), search locations by proximity or keyword search, and access receipts.

V2 is scheduled for January and includes real-time customer data for checked in locations, making friends on the app, and list of favorite locations.

V3 is scheduled for April and includes special events, exclusives deals, split payment, Uber/Lyft integration and personal drink recommendations.
