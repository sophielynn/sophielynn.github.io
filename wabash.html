Landmarks


While sitting in between two biggest streets in Chicago, Wabash Avenue is under a train track which makes it noisy, dark, dirty and the least place people would like to hang out in the Loop. As a next strategic move to renovate the Loop in Chicago, how to make Wabash a destination in the lenses of tourism becomes our topic.

We work with Chicago Loop Alliance and also many other related associations. With tons of research, analysis and prototypes, we succeed with helping them to vision this posibility.

This is Oscar and his family, the mother Carrie and the 15-year-old daughter Haley.
They come to Chicago for a spring break trip from Wisconsin.

SCENE 01


It's a great morning, and they come to the Cloud Gate (also known as the Bean).
It is a must-go-and-see place for most tourists in Chicago.


When they approaching, Haley finds a sign painted on the ground

move your mouse to see the sign


They feel interesting about this quiz and start finding the tallest building.
they spend a great time and take a lot of pictures as well which in the meanwhile, they find another clue on the ground.

move your mouse to find out the answer and next clue


They are really curious about this "dark" place. So they follow the path.

SCENE 02


They come to an alley where there are music, food trucks and city seats. A Chicago style hotdog cures their hunger and tiredness.
They also find so many people taking pictures with the interactive mural and so do they.

move your mouse to find out the next clue


They keep heading along the sign on the column and they find Wabash Light just near by.

SCENE 03


Led by the sign, they come to the famouse Palmer House. Fabulous decorations are right in front of them.

move your mouse to see the sign


They look over and over and finally, Haley screams out, "look, oh deer!"

move your mouse to find out the answer and next clue


They keep walking and start to feel a little bit tired as time goes by.

SCENE 04


They come to a photo booth where it provides a great angle to the view of the ART INSTITUTE OF Chicago.
Haley is excited and immediately takes a photo which she would definitely get hundreds of likes on Instagram.

move your mouse to check out the picture they take


They keep following the marks on the wall which lead them to the Macy's.

SCENE 05


They follow the sign all the way to the 7th floor of Macy's where they find the Chocolate Chicago,
a small scall of Chicago made with chocolate by artists. Haley take a picture of all those sweets.
and it also reminds them to treat themselves since they are all tired.

SCENE 06


They follow the clue to a 2nd floor cafe where there are food, drinks, seats, games and free wifi.
What's adding to it is the great view to the train
Oscar and Carrie take a seat, have a coffee and enjoy the sunshine of afternoon,
while Haley is busying retouching all her pictures today which she will show off on Instagram.
